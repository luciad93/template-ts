User Guide

Button Functions:

  • L - Light: Toggles the backlight of the watch display.
  • M - Mode: Cycles through the modes: View Time, Edit Hours, Edit Minutes.
  • I - Increase: In Edit Hours mode, increases the hour. In Edit Minutes mode, increases the minute.
  • R - Reset: Resets the current setting operation.
  • F - Format: Toggles between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats.

Timeouts: After a period of inactivity in Edit mode, the watch will automatically return to View Time mode to save power and simplify usage. The light will also turn off automatically after a timeout.
